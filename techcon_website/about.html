Our History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small developer meetup and has grown into a major global tech conference that brings together thousands of professionals to share ideas,
trends, and breakthroughs.

Mission

Innovation – Encourage new ideas and breakthrough technologies.
Collaboration – Bring together diverse voices from across tech fields.
Inclusion – Promote accessibility and diversity in tech communities.
Education – Provide learning opportunities through talks, workshops, and panels.
Impact – Drive positive change in industries, economies, and societies through technology.

Each year, TechCon features top minds in tech. Here are a few highlight.

Dr. Nia Grant

A renowned data scientist and AI researcher, Dr. Grant has led cutting-edge work in machine learning at several top universities.
Her 2022 TechCon keynote focused on building transparent, bias-free AI systems for healthcare and education.

Ethan Zhao

As co-founder of QuantumLeap Technologies, Ethan is a trailblazer in quantum computing.
At TechCon 2021, he unveiled a prototype that significantly accelerated complex simulations for climate research.
